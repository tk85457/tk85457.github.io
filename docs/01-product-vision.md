# SyncDub AI Product Bible

# Chapter 01 — Product Vision

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision, principles, goals, success metrics, and product philosophy for SyncDub AI. It is the primary source of truth for every future product, design, engineering, marketing, and AI-agent decision.

## Vision

Enable anyone to watch any video, course, movie, livestream, or presentation in their own language with natural AI speech and minimal latency.

## Mission

Build the world's most trusted real-time AI voice translation platform.

## Product Principles

- Speed before complexity.
- Reliability before new features.
- Natural listening over artificial effects.
- Invisible user experience.
- Honest communication.
- Accessibility by default.

## Core User Promise

Install the extension.
Choose your language.
Press play.
Forget language barriers.

## Long-Term Direction

- Browser extension
- Desktop apps
- Mobile apps
- Public API
- Enterprise platform
- Creator tools
- Live meeting translation
- AI speech infrastructure

## Success Metrics

- Translation success rate
- Playback continuity
- Average latency
- Customer retention
- Credit conversion
- Crash-free sessions

## Living Document

This chapter is intentionally versioned and will expand over time as SyncDub AI evolves. Future chapters reference this document instead of redefining product goals.
