# SyncDub AI Product Bible

# Chapter 07 — Dashboard Specification

## Objective
The dashboard is the operational center of SyncDub AI where users manage credits, sessions, billing, and account settings.

## Main Sections
- Overview
- Credits
- Recent Sessions
- Usage Analytics
- Purchase History
- Billing
- Devices
- Account
- Settings
- Support

## Dashboard Principles
- Surface important information first.
- Minimize navigation depth.
- Use progressive disclosure for advanced settings.
- Make credit balance visible at all times.

## Widgets
- Credit balance
- Usage chart
- Recent translations
- Quick purchase button
- Latest activity

## Empty States
Provide clear guidance when no sessions or purchases exist.

## Success Metric
Users should complete common account tasks in fewer than three interactions.
