# SyncDub AI Product Bible

# Chapter 02 — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Position

SyncDub AI is a premium productivity product focused on removing language barriers through real-time AI voice translation. Every visual decision should reinforce trust, clarity, speed, and craftsmanship.

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Intelligent
- Professional
- Reliable
- Minimal
- Human

## Design Principles

1. Simplicity over decoration.
2. Motion must communicate state.
3. Typography drives hierarchy.
4. Waveform is the signature brand element.
5. Consistency is more important than novelty.

## Visual Language

- Dark-first interface.
- Premium purple and blue accents.
- Soft lighting.
- Minimal glass usage.
- Large whitespace.
- Rounded components.

## Typography

Primary: Inter Variable.
Fallback: System UI.

## Iconography

Use one consistent icon family across the product.
Avoid mixing icon packs.

## Motion

Animations should explain transitions, loading, playback, and connection states. Never animate for decoration alone.

## Brand Voice

Clear.
Confident.
Helpful.
Never exaggerated.
Never misleading.

## Brand Consistency

The website, extension, dashboard, and future apps must feel like parts of the same ecosystem through shared spacing, typography, colors, motion, and waveform identity.
